Biography

I am a researcher at Purdue University focused on the intersection of robotics, neuromorphic vision, and multimodal perception. My work centers on developing intelligent systems that can "see" and "sense" in challenging environments using unconventional sensors. Specifically, I specialize in mmWave radar calibration and object detection, as well as event-based cameras for high-speed neuromorphic vision. I leverage Reinforcement Learning and Control Theory to bridge the gap between complex perception and robotic locomotion.
